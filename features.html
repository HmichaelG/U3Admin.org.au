Why U3Admin?

U3Admin is a comprehensive, cloud-based solution designed specifically for University of the Third Age organizations. Built from the ground up with input from U3A administrators and members, our software streamlines every aspect of U3A management.

Whether you're managing 50 members or 5,000, U3Admin scales with your needs while remaining intuitive and easy to use.

Core Features

Member Portal

Members access their U3A membership and enrolment details anywhere, anytime, on any device. Robust security ensures access is granted to their details and only their details.

Learn More →

Administration Website

Add courses, change class times, review statistics, or run reports with ease. Contextual help is always just a click away. Secure, role-based access ensures data integrity.

Learn More →

Automated Workflows

Our system works 24/7 to email receipts, confirm enrolments, send class lists to leaders, prepare attendance records, and manage waitlists automatically.

Open Source & Free

U3Admin is open source and not-for-profit. Free to download and customize. We don't sell software—it's free for all U3A organizations.

View on GitHub

Reliable Email

Say goodbye to bounced emails. Our world-class email service provider delivers 99.9% successful delivery rates, ensuring your communications reach members.

Secure Data

Encrypted in transit and at rest. Your data is kept in a locked box, safe from prying eyes yet immediately available to authorized users.

Australian Hosted

Your website and data are hosted locally in Australia within Microsoft Azure data centers, ensuring compliance and fast performance.

Scales With You

Whether your U3A is large or small, U3Admin is a great fit. Our software handles organizations from 50 to 5,000+ members with equal ease.
